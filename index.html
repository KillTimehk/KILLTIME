KILLTIME helps tertiary education students to spend their time wisely by creating a platform.

We welcome you to join our platform and reach over 290,000 students in Hong Kong.

Details in PDF

SHARING

Students can share images and videos on this map-based platform. Not only share their enjoyable life, but also gain insights on how other students live their lives. Different from other social networking sites, students can post things anonymously, and their posts will stay in their personal profile.

GIVING

This function is to match students who have extra time and students who need help. Students can request for help or give their helping hands to others. Students can request for help in two categories, Academics and others. They have to specify the location and descriptions in order to request for help.

EXPERIENCING

Students can search for shops or leisure activities which provide student discounts, and get the discounts through the application.
